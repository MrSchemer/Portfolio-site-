With a profound passion for physiology, biotechnology, genetics, genomics, pathology, sustainable agriculture, and food security, he dives deep into the agricultural sciences. His dedication is evident through completing two action research and his coauthorship in over ten research and review papers. Adding to his impressive portfolio, he has published two research papers showcasing his commitment to advancing knowledge and innovation in his field. He actively participates in different national and international summits and conferences.

Besides this, he is the author of Secrets of Applied Science Entrance Preparation Book from Samiksha Publication, and Booster Knowledge Book from Grade 1 to Grade 7 from Megha Publication. He has recently published ‘A Complete Manual of Plant and Animal Science’ for the first and second semesters of the Diploma in Agriculture CTEVT course. His other book ‘A Complete Manual of Plant Science’ for the third, fourth, fifth, and sixth semesters of the Diploma in Agriculture CTEVT course from Samiksha Publication is on the verge of publication.

Moreover, His journey from Rasuwa district second topper in SEE 2075, Merit-based scholarship by Kathmandu Institute of Science and Technology (KIST) College (2019-2021), the KIST Scholar Award 2022, the Nandalal Jagateswori Award 2022, Merit-based Scholarship by Agriculture and Forestry University (2022-2026), University Grant Commission (UGC) Scholarship (2022-2026) by the government of Nepal to the Golden Jubilee Scholarship (2023-2026) by the Embassy of India have proven his academic brilliance.

He actively engages in various organizations that are aligned with their values and interests. He is the Campus Ambassador of Eco Network Global, University Ambassador of G-17 Nepal, Volunteer in Nepalese Youth for Climate Action (NYCA)-Rampur, member of Nepalese Agriculture Professional of America (NAPA), Global outreach student member of the American Society of Microbiology, and Treasurer of Bagmati Student Family in AFU.
